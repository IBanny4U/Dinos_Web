Our Vision

At The Dino’s Burmese Traditional Food, our vision is to show the world how delicious Burmese food truly is. We aim to celebrate the heritage and cultures of Myanmar’s diverse ethnic groups while bringing attention to the country’s underrated cuisines.

Through our hard work and dedication, we hope to earn Burmese food a special place in the hearts of people worldwide. Our website is a reflection of this vision—a platform to share the flavors, stories, and traditions that make Myanmar’s cuisine unique and unforgettable.

Join us on this journey to explore the delicious diversity of Burmese food and culture!

About Members

Our team consists of U Chit Mg, Mura, Rainn, Ennara, NayKyar, Mercury, Mint, Mink, IBanny, and Gwenn. Within our group, we have three key roles: data collectors, designers, and website developers.

Each of us contributed by gathering recipes, while Mint and Mink led the design process for the website. Using GitHub, we collaborated as a team to bring this project to life. The majority of the recipe data came from the internet and social media, with a few recipes being our own creations. Photos were sourced from Pinterest, Facebook, and Google, and tutorial videos came from YouTube. We’ve ensured to credit all the original creators and sources.

Together, we’re proud to share this project with you, showcasing our passion for Burmese food and culture!

Discover authentic Burmese food recipe, featuring popular dishes, traditional snacks, and flavorful meals from Myanmar. Perfect for food enthusiasts!
